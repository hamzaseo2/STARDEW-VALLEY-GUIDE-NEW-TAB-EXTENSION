### ⚡ Pro Tips — Beginner to Expert

**Year 1 Priority Checklist:**
- [ ] Day 1: Plant Parsnips immediately
- [ ] Week 1: Upgrade backpack (2,000g from Pierre)
- [ ] Spring: Fish daily for extra gold income
- [ ] Spring: Buy Strawberry seeds at Egg Festival (Day 13)
- [ ] Summer: Plant Starfruit — start building Kegs
- [ ] Fall: Plant Cranberries and Pumpkins
- [ ] Winter: Upgrade tools, socialise, mine for gold

**Top 10 Must-Know Tips:**

1. **Plant on Day 1** — Every day you delay costs you a harvest cycle
2. **Build Kegs first** — They triple crop value and are your main income source
3. **Upgrade Watering Can to Gold** — Waters 3×3 area, saves massive energy
4. **Upgrade backpack early** — 36 slots = more items per mining/foraging trip
5. **Always ship crops** — Shipping Box pays full price; Pierre pays less
6. **Give 2 gifts per week** — Max out all villager relationships for rewards
7. **Never pass out at 2 AM** — You lose gold and dropped items
8. **Complete Community Center** — Greenhouse lets you farm crops in winter
9. **Forage every day** — Ground items disappear after one day
10. **Fish on lucky days** — Check TV each morning for Fortune Teller forecast
